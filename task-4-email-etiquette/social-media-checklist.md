# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
1. Keep your profiles professional and respectful.
2. Think before posting anything publicly.
3. Use strong passwords for all social media accounts.
4. Verify information before sharing it.
5. Use privacy settings wisely.

## Don’ts
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
3. Do not click suspicious links or messages.
4. Do not share OTPs, passwords, or private details online.
5. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments unnecessarily.
