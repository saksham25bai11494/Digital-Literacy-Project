# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or bank password with anyone.
2. Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.
3. Always verify payment requests before approving them.
4. Use only official apps and websites for banking or payments.
5. Avoid clicking suspicious links received by SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
6. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
7. Check the sender’s identity before trusting urgent payment messages.
8. Keep your phone and apps updated for better security.
9. For UPI safety, remember that receiving money does not require entering your PIN.
10. For financial safety, never trust “refund” or “cashback” calls without verification.
